About Me

Hello there, and welcome to my digital portfolio. My name is Gerald Dewitt-Smith. I am a student at the University of Connecticut in Stamford, CT. Currently enrolled in my second year majoring in the Digital Media and Design program with a concentration in 2D Animation. One of my biggest goals/dreams is to one day work in the film or television business. I have a small amount of experience in digital media creation and I have a lot to learn, but I'm willing to put in the effort to reach my full potential. I'm expected to graduate in the spring of 2020.
